Our Story

Founded in 2016 by Kirk Griffin Tan, Korbie's Kitchen began as a humble food truck, serving up delicious, handcrafted meals to local communities. What started with a passion for cooking and a dream to share great food has blossomed into a beloved restaurant known for its commitment to quality and flavor. From our early days serving wagyu beef burgers and gourmet hotdogs curbside, to expanding into a full-service restaurant, our journey has been fueled by a love for creating memorable dining experiences. Each recipe is crafted with care, using the freshest ingredients sourced locally whenever possible. Today, Korbie's Kitchen stands as a testament to our dedication to food excellence and customer satisfaction. We invite you to join us on this culinary adventure and taste the passion in every bite.

Our Mission

At Korbie's Kitchen, our mission is simple: to delight our guests with exceptional food and service. We strive to create a welcoming atmosphere where friends and family can gather to enjoy delicious meals made with integrity and love. From sourcing ingredients responsibly to preparing each dish with precision and care, we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of quality. We believe that good food brings people together and aim to foster community through our shared love of great cuisine. As we continue to grow, our mission remains rooted in providing a dining experience that exceeds expectations. Whether you're stopping by for a quick bite or celebrating a special occasion, we look forward to serving you with warmth and hospitality.
